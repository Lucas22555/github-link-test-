Shanghai Tower

The Shanghai Tower is a striking skyscraper located in Shanghai, China. Completed in 2015, it stands as the tallest building in China and the second-tallest in the world, reaching a height of 632 meters (2,073 feet). Designed by the architectural firm Gensler, the tower's unique spiral form and sustainable design have garnered international acclaim. It houses a variety of functions, including office space, hotels, retail outlets, and observation decks. The Shanghai Tower serves as a symbol of Shanghai's economic prowess and modern urban development, contributing to the city's iconic skyline.
